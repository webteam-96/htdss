Our three pillars

Education · Enrichment · Empowerment

Education

Sharing the latest in diabetes care with physicians, nurses and students through conferences, workshops and webinars.

Enrichment

Building a richer clinical community in Howrah — diabetes specialists, endocrinologists and family physicians together.

Empowerment

Awareness camps, walkathons and free screenings so the community can take charge of its own health.
